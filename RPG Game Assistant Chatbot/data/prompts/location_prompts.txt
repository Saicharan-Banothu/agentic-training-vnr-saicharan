Generate a detailed location for the game. Include:

1. NAME: An evocative name for the location
2. TYPE: What kind of place it is (settlement, dungeon, natural feature, etc.)
3. DESCRIPTION: Vivid sensory details (sights, sounds, smells)
4. HISTORY: Brief background or lore
5. INHABITANTS: Who or what can be found here
6. POINTS OF INTEREST: 3-5 notable features or areas
7. DANGERS/OPPORTUNITIES: Potential threats or rewards
8. GAME MECHANICS: Any relevant rules or mechanics for this location

Create a location that feels alive and offers gameplay opportunities.
